# Hospital-Emergency-Room-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI & SQL)

## Project Objective
The objective of the Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard is to analyze patient flow, wait times, admission trends, and overall hospital performance. This dashboard helps healthcare professionals monitor emergency room efficiency, improve patient care, and optimize resource allocation.

## ❓ Key Questions (KPIs)
- What is the **total number of patients visiting the emergency room**?
- What is the **average patient wait time**?
- What is the **patient satisfaction score**?
- How many **patients are referred to other departments**?
- What is the **admission vs non-admission rate**?
- What percentage of **patients are seen within 30 minutes**?
- How are **patients distributed by age group**?
- What is the **gender distribution of patients**?
- How are **patients distributed across departments (referrals)**?
- What are the **peak hours and days for patient visits**?

## 🔄 Process
- Extracted and validated hospital data using SQL queries.
- Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query.
- Created DAX measures such as:
  - Total Patients  
  - Average Wait Time  
  - Patient Satisfaction Score  
  - Referral Count  
- Built interactive dashboard visuals including:
  - Patient trend (monthly & consolidated view)  
  - Admission status breakdown  
  - Patients seen within target time  
  - Age group distribution  
  - Gender distribution  
  - Department referral analysis  
  - Day & hour heatmap for patient flow  
- Applied slicers for dynamic filtering (Month, Date Range).

## 🔍 Project Insights
- **Total patients reached 9K+**, showing high hospital traffic in the emergency department.
- **Average wait time is around 35 minutes**, indicating moderate patient handling efficiency.
- **Patient satisfaction score is high (~4.6–4.9)**, reflecting good service quality.
- Around **50% of patients are admitted**, showing balanced admission rates.
- Approximately **59% of patients are treated within 30 minutes**, meeting service targets.
- **Adults (20–40 age group)** form the majority of ER visits.
- **Gender distribution is nearly equal**, with slight variation across time periods.
- **General Practice and Orthopedics** receive the highest patient referrals.
- Peak patient traffic occurs during **mid-day and evening hours**, especially on weekdays.

## ✅ Final Conclusion  
The **Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard** provides critical insights into patient flow, service efficiency, and hospital performance. It enables healthcare providers to make data-driven decisions by identifying bottlenecks, improving response times, and enhancing overall patient experience.
